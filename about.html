Every Time Machine Needs a License Plate

The internet is a funny place. It's more about throughput than persistence. That's why the Internet Archive has a Wayback Machine: to show us where we've been, lest we forget where we're going.

This is the hub of my personal time machine, and DR3WH0 is its license plate.
